## Changing plans

Manage your plan from the **Settings** page at [app.openlegion.ai](https://app.openlegion.ai).

### Upgrades

Upgrades take effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period. Your agent and project limits increase right away.

If the upgrade requires a larger server, an automatic resize happens — this takes about a minute of downtime.

### Downgrades

Downgrades are scheduled for the end of your current billing period. You keep your current plan's limits until then. At renewal, your plan switches and limits adjust.

You can cancel a pending downgrade from the Settings page by clicking **Keep current plan**.

### Billing interval changes

Switching between monthly and yearly billing takes effect at your next renewal.

## Cancellation

Cancel from the **Settings** page. Your instance stays active until the end of your current billing period. After that, the server is deprovisioned and data is deleted.

You can **resume** a cancelled subscription at any time before the billing period ends.

## Payment issues

If a payment fails, your subscription enters a **past due** state. You'll see a banner in the app dashboard prompting you to update your payment method. After 3 days, your instance is paused. After 14 days without payment, the instance is permanently deleted.
