Sign up at [app.openlegion.ai](https://app.openlegion.ai) and get a dedicated agent fleet with zero infrastructure setup.

## Sign up and subscribe

<Steps>
<Step title="Create an account">

Go to [app.openlegion.ai](https://app.openlegion.ai) and sign in with **Google**, **GitHub**, or **Discord**.

</Step>

<Step title="Choose a plan">

After signing in, you'll see the pricing page. Pick the plan that fits your needs:

| Plan | Price | Agents | Browsers | Projects |
|------|-------|--------|----------|----------|
| **Basic** | $19/mo | 1 | 1 | — |
| **Growth** | $49/mo | 5 | 5 | 2 |
| **Pro** | $119/mo | 15 | 10 | 5 |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

All plans include container-isolated execution, 100+ LLM providers, a monitoring dashboard, and a custom subdomain. Yearly billing saves 25%.

Click **Get Started** on your chosen plan to complete checkout.

</Step>

<Step title="Choose your subdomain">

After payment, you'll land on the setup page. Pick a subdomain for your fleet — this becomes your permanent URL:

```
https://my-fleet.engine.openlegion.ai
```

Subdomains must be 3-20 characters, start with a letter, and can contain lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens.

Click **Launch My Server**.

</Step>

<Step title="Wait for provisioning">

Your dedicated server is being set up. The setup page shows live progress through four stages:

1. **Creating your dedicated server** — a VPS is provisioned in the cloud
2. **Installing OpenLegion** — Docker, the engine, and all dependencies are installed
3. **Configuring your instance** — your fleet is configured and started
4. **Go live** — your instance is ready

This typically takes 10-15 minutes. The page updates automatically.

</Step>

<Step title="Open your dashboard">

Once provisioning completes, click **Open Dashboard** to access your fleet's engine dashboard. From there you can:

- Add API keys for your LLM providers
- Configure agents (personalities, tools, budgets)
- Connect messaging channels (Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp)
- Set up workflows and automation
- Monitor costs and activity in real time

</Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Your fleet runs on a dedicated server at `{your-subdomain}.engine.openlegion.ai`. You manage it through the engine dashboard — the same interface available to self-hosted users.

The app at [app.openlegion.ai](https://app.openlegion.ai) handles your subscription, billing, and server management. The engine dashboard on your subdomain is where you configure and interact with your agents.
